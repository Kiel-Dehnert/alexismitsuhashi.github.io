Inworks Branding and Illustrations

Inworks is a Human-Centered Design school at the University of Colorado Denver. As their graphic illustrator, I was tasked with creating brand illustrations depicting their four tenets: design thinking, computational thinking, disruptive innovation, and radical interdisciplinarity. My process used definitions from the Inworks white paper and interviews with faculty and students. You can see them on the live Inworks site here .
